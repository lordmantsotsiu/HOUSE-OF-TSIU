# House Of Tšiu · The Codex of Self

**An ultra-luxury digital sanctuary** — where daily wisdom meets old-money ethos. A web experience that blends secretive elegance with raw, unyielding principles: Honour, Legacy, and Dominance.

> *“Knowledge is the only lineage that never lies. Nurture it, and you become your own dynasty.”*

---

## ✦ Overview

House Of Tšiu is not merely a quote generator. It is a **ritual**. Every day, the platform delivers a unique, powerful verse rooted in one of three cardinal virtues. Before engaging with the quotes, the user encounters a philosophical manifesto on why knowledge shapes an individual more than any material fortune — a nod to the old-money belief that intellect is the ultimate heirloom.

The interface is minimal, austere, and cinematic: solid black backgrounds, gilded accents, serif typography, and blurred glass panels. It feels like a private club’s reading room — secretive, weighty, and reserved for those who seek self-mastery.

---

## ✦ Core Features

### 1. Daily Deterministic Quotes
- Each quote is bound to the **UTC date**. The verse changes every sunrise.
- Three distinct virtue paths:
  - **Honour** – integrity, loyalty, unforgiving memory of betrayal.
  - **Legacy** – dynasty, eternal impact, building beyond one lifetime.
  - **Dominance** – quiet authority, strategic caution, absolute consequence for treachery.

### 2. Philosophical Prelude
Before selection, an introductory section explains why **daily lessons and knowledge** surpass gold in shaping an individual. This sets the tone: you are not here for motivation — you are here for formation.

### 3. Real‑Time Conglomerate Chart
A live‑updated bar chart displays the top 10 private conglomerates in the world (Koch Industries, Cargill, Vitol, Huawei, IKEA, etc.). The data refreshes every 15 seconds, simulating market dynamics. This reinforces the old‑money theme: awareness of private power structures.

### 4. Cinematic Time & Date
- Live digital clock with gilded gradient.
- Full date displayed in an elegant, under‑stated capsule.

### 5. Ultra‑Luxury Aesthetic
- **Solid black background** (`#000000`) – no gradients, no distractions.
- **Gold/bronze accent palette** – `#cfb87a`, `#cdb27a`, `#e2cd94`.
- **Glassmorphism panels** – blurred, semi‑transparent cards with fine borders.
- **Custom serif typography** – Cormorant Garamond for headings, Inter for body.
- **No emojis** – only typographic symbols (⚜, ◈, 🗡) for a high‑class feel.
